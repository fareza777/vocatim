# Vocatim Play Store Promo — Design

## Objective

Create a 30-second, 16:9 product-promo video for Vocatim that feels calm,
premium, and credible. It should communicate the product's on-device
transcription, organization, and AI-assistance value without generic
"AI magic" effects or a voice-over.

## Creative direction

**Quiet Confidence.** The film uses the real Vocatim interface as evidence of
the product. Motion is restrained: slow framing, precise crops, clean type,
and a subtle audio bed. The dark ink, violet, and teal product palette carries
through the whole piece.

The video must not claim that data never leaves the device, because Vocatim
also offers an optional BYOK cloud-AI path. Copy instead highlights
"Transcribe on-device" and ends with the established brand line:
"Offline. Private. Yours."

## Production approach

- Use existing, genuine Vocatim screenshots as product footage. Do not fake
  the application UI or show features that do not exist.
- Compose 1920×1080 video at 30 fps with H.264 MP4 delivery.
- Use the app's dark visual language for continuity, including ink surfaces,
  violet/teal accents, generous negative space, and editorial typography.
- Create animation through camera movement, masks, waveform motion, timing,
  and controlled text transitions. Avoid stock-template transitions, particle
  effects, exaggerated glows, or fake device rotations.
- Include no voice-over. Use an original minimal ambient/sound-design bed only;
  no licensed third-party music is required.

## Success criteria

- Looks like a premium product film, not a feature slideshow.
- Vocatim branding and product claims remain accurate.
- Copy stays legible at standard YouTube/Play Store viewing size.
- UI screenshots stay crisp and are never distorted.
- Final runtime is 30 seconds (+/- 0.5 seconds) and the MP4 plays locally.
