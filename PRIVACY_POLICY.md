# Vocatim Privacy Policy

*Last updated: 4 July 2026*

Vocatim is built so that your data stays yours. This policy is short because there is genuinely nothing to disclose.

## What we collect
**Nothing.** Vocatim does not collect, transmit, store remotely, or share any personal data, audio, transcripts, usage analytics, or device identifiers.

## How your data is handled
- **Audio recordings and transcripts** are processed and stored only on your device, in the app's private storage. They never leave your phone unless you explicitly share or export them yourself.
- **Speech recognition** runs entirely on-device using the Whisper model. No audio or text is sent to any server.
- **Internet access** is used for exactly two things: downloading the speech model files (from Hugging Face) on first use, and Google Play Billing when you choose to purchase. Neither involves your recordings or transcripts.
- **Purchases** are processed by Google Play. We receive no personal information from the transaction.

## Permissions
- **Microphone** — to record audio, only while you use the record feature.
- **Notifications** — to show recording/transcription progress.

## Data deletion
Deleting a transcript in the app permanently removes it and its audio file. Uninstalling the app removes all app data.

## Children
Vocatim does not collect data from anyone, including children.
